Tzatziki

A light and refreshing dip for hot summer days

This mediteranean appetizer dish is great for hot days, packed with light and refreshing ingredients this is a great mid day snack! Best enjoyed with some fresh pita bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain Greek yogurt (optional - strain with cheese cloth)
  • 1 cup shredded or diced cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice or white wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped dill
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
